# King of Time Travel
A Unity‑built 2D adventure platformer where you play as a time‑traveling King, exploring medieval castle rooms and futuristic space‑station chambers. Collect keystones, unlock doors, defeat enemies with your royal hammer, and avoid deadly hazards as you search for the hidden treasure.

## Gameplay Overview🚀
In this adventure, you travel through a series of interconnected rooms that shift between medieval castles and futuristic space‑station corridors. Each area contains keystones that must be collected to unlock new doors and progress deeper into the world. Along the way, you’ll encounter enemies that can be defeated by jumping on them with your hammer, as well as environmental hazards such as pools of green burning liquid. The game blends platforming challenges with light combat and exploration across two different time periods.

## Controls 🎮
Arrow Keys — Move left and right
Up Arrow — Enter doors / interact
Spacebar — Jump
Hammer Jump — Jump onto enemies to defeat them

## Features ✨
King of Time Travel includes a variety of mechanics designed to create a fun and challenging 2D platforming experience. Players can explore both castle and space‑station environments, each with unique layouts and hazards. The keystone and door system adds progression and exploration, while the hammer‑jump mechanic provides a simple but satisfying way to defeat enemies. Environmental traps, animated transitions, and a mix of fantasy and sci‑fi themes help bring the world to life.
